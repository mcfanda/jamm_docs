Rosetta store: results across software

(Marcello Gallucci)

Comparisons with other software: Here you can find comparisons of results obtained in jamovi jAMM, and other software, usually jamovi (jmv), pure R, and SPSS. The pages are primarily meant to test convergence between jAMM and well-established packages, but they can be useful also to check how to get equivalent results across different software.
